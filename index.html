About Ascent Physiotherapy

At Ascent Physiotherapy in Comox, BC, we're your dedicated partners in achieving optimal health. Our comprehensive physiotherapy services include Manual/Manipulative Therapy, IMS, Acupuncture, Radial Shockwave Therapy, and personalized exercise prescription, including Personal Training. Serving the vibrant community of the Comox Valley,
Our mission is to empower individuals on their journey to better health. We deliver expert care, foster a supportive environment, and tailor treatment plans to meet your unique needs. Join us as we elevate your well-being for a healthier, more active lifestyle.
