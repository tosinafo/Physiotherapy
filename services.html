Our Specialized Services

Manual/Manipulative Therapy

At Ascent Physiotherapy, our Manual/Manipulative Therapy is like a tailored dance for your joints. Our skilled therapists use precise, hands-on movements to bring back harmony to your musculoskeletal system. Whether you're dealing with joint pain, stiffness, or limited movement, this therapy is your personalized symphony of relief.

Radial Shockwave Therapy

Imagine your body healing with the power of sound. Our Radial Shockwave Therapy sends ripples of energy through injured tissues, awakening your body's natural healing prowess. It's like a gentle yet powerful massage for deep-seated issues, providing relief for chronic tendonitis and musculoskeletal disorders.

Acupuncture

Step into the ancient art of healing with our Acupuncture services. It's like finding the rhythm in your body's energy flow. Tiny needles create a symphony of balance, addressing musculoskeletal pain, headaches, and stress-related concerns. Experience the ancient wisdom tailored to your modern well-being.

Physical Training

Get ready for a personalized fitness journey that's all about you. Our Exercise Prescription, including Personal Training, is like having a fitness concierge. Tailored exercises, designed just for you, guided by our certified trainers. It's not just about movement; it's about your unique path to strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.
